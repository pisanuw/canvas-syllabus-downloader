The information on <http://courses.washington.edu/css497/>provides a general outline of the format and deliverables for your capstone. However, each capstone has its own unique flavor and I will work with you in adjusting the deliverables as needed. 

Throughout your capstone, you will submit weekly or bi-weekly Reports (see Assignments section). The Reports are due by Sunday midnight. These reports will be combined and included as part of your Final Report.

After completing your capstone, you will present your poster and give a public presentation. The Colloquium is typically on the Friday of finals week. 

### **Overall Structure**

  1. Upload your signed contract to Canvas  
  

  2. Submit your Reports regularly, organize a meeting with me if needed. If you are doing a project with me, we should meet regularly. Email me to setup a time as necessary.  
  

  3. 14 days prior to the colloquium, submit Abstract, Draft presentation slides and Draft poster  
  

  4. We will hold a practice presentation ~10 days before the colloquium. Usually the Wednesday the week before the colloquium.  
  

  5. Submit your Final Report before the colloquium  
  

  6. The below resources provide general guidance for your deliverables



Janet will create a separate Canvas course to collect abstracts, posters and presentations for the colloquium. You will need to submit those items to the other course as well as to this course.
