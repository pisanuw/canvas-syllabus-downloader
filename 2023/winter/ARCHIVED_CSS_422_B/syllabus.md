## **Course Information**

 

This is an introductory course into the fundamentals of digital hardware, the architecture of modern microprocessors and assembly language programming. This course will provide students with the basic theories and concepts of how hardware and software cooperatively interact to accomplish real-world tasks. (more details in **[syllabus, click here](https://canvas.uw.edu/courses/1608378/pages/syllabus "Syllabus"))**.
